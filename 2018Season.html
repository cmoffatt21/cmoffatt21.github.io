2018-2019 Season

The CMS Stags won their first SCIAC Title in over 30 years in 2018 and made their first ever playoff appearance. The Stags relied heavily on junior running back, Garret Cheadle, who broke the college record for rushing yards. Additionally, the Stag defense was the strongest in the conference and consistently put the offense in the best position to win games.

CMS finished the season 6-1 in conference allowing less than 300 yards a game. Additionally, the Stags led the nation in time of possession thanks to a dominant offensive line and Defense.
